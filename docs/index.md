## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the scanner work?
Beanfolio uses AI to extract information from the front and back of coffee bags. For the best results, ensure the bag is well-lit and the text is clearly visible in the frame.

### Can I edit the information after scanning?
Yes! After scanning, you will be taken to a review screen where you can tap any field to edit the extracted details before saving them to your collection.

### Where is my data stored?
Your coffee collection is securely stored in the cloud using Firebase, which means you can access it from any device where you are signed in with the same account.
